How It Works

For Entrepreneurs

  1. Sign Up & Build Your Profile: Create your account and detail your company's mission and vision.
  2. Submit Your Pitch: Fill out our structured form to clearly present your business idea, plan, and funding requirements.
  3. Engage with Investors: Answer questions publicly on our Q&A platform and receive private offers directly to your dashboard.
  4. Accept & Connect: Accept the right offer and open a private chat to discuss the next steps towards partnership.

For Investors

  1. Register & Define Your Thesis: Join the network and specify your investment interests to find relevant opportunities.
  2. Browse & Filter Pitches: Use our search and filter tools to discover startups that match your criteria.
  3. Perform Due Diligence: Ask clarifying questions on the public Q&A and review detailed company profiles.
  4. Make an Offer: Submit a formal offer directly through the platform and begin a private conversation upon acceptance.
